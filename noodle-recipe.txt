Ingredients:
 - 1 package soba noodles
 - 1 teaspoon sesame oil
 - 3 tablespoons soy sauce
 - 1 teaspoon hot chili oil
 - 5 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
 - 1 carrot
 - thinly sliced or julienned
 - 2 celery stalks
 - 1/2 cup thinly sliced napa cabbage
 - 5 green onions
 - 1/2 cup julienned bok choy
 - 3 tablespoons minced fresh cilantro leaves
 - 3 tablespoons sesame seeds, toasted, for garnish
 - 4 tablespoons unsalted peanuts, for garnish

Directions:
 1. In a medium stock pot, boil water, add salt and cook noodles. When finished, 
    place noodles in an ice water bath to cool. Drain and set aside.
 
 2. In a medium bowl combine, sesame oil, vinegar, soy sauce, hot chili oil, 
    hoisin and extra-virgin olive oil. 
 
 3. Mix thoroughly and then combine prepared vegetables and noodles.
    Garnish with sesame seeds and peanuts.
